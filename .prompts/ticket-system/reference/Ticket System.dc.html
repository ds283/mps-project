3Routing model — convenor triage & office compose

Working through routing. A ticket carries one or more subjects — students and/or project classes — and the set of project classes “in scope” is the union of any pinned classes plus the home class of every attached student. Auto-assignment rule: exactly one class in scope → auto-assigned to that class’s convenor; more than one (or none pinned to a class) → left unassigned and surfaced on every relevant convenor’s dashboard for triage. Tickets may be unassigned and may name several students/classes at once. 3a is the convenor’s triage view of incoming routed tickets; 3b is the office compose form with its wider, multi-select scope.
